About

I've been working as a product designer for over 5 years, mostly on complex digital products where the real challenge isn't making things pretty but making them work. Media platforms, fintech, high-traffic apps.

I tend to show up when a product has grown faster than its structure and someone needs to bring order to it. I like getting into the complexity: messy flows, large-scale data, cross-functional teams with real constraints. I work fast, I get along well with data, and the more context I have, the better.

I've worked with teams across different countries, always in English. I integrate AI tools into my daily workflow to unblock, document, and move faster. Open to remote roles worldwide.
